About Me

Hello hello! My name is Luke Arnold, and I'm currently a sophmore at Northwestern University majoring in Computer Science and Theater. Growing up in the Bay Area, I had the opportunity to start coding early in high school, and have been in love ever since. My experience is primarily in Python and C/C++, but I love learning new languages, and am especialy interested in the ways that computers can be used to extend and enhance human ability. I get easily excited about cool ideas, and can often be found standing on tables, chairs, or anything else that's not the ground when brainstorming and planning projects.

I'm a big fan of interesting tech (and human achievement in general), and love watching video essays in my spare time. I also have done a lot of educational work with kids ages 5-12, and have developed an enthusiastic appreciation for their often unorthodox thinking and perspectives, as well as a deep respect for their boundless curiosity. You always think you understand something until you try to explain it to a five year old!
